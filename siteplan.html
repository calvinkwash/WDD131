Site Purpose

This website offers a curated selection of sustainable fashion for eco-conscious consumers. It will include product listings, materials transparency, sizing information, and a checkout system. It also promotes ethical brands and educates visitors on sustainable living.

Planned Content

The website will showcase a curated collection of sustainable and eco-friendly fashion products, including clothing, accessories, and footwear. It will feature:

  • Product pages with images, descriptions, prices, and customer reviews
  • Information about sustainable fashion principles
  • Shipping and return policies
  • Contact and support information

Reason for Choosing This Subject

I am passionate about sustainability and fashion, and I want to create a website that promotes eco-friendly shopping options. This project will help me practice building a user-friendly, responsive e-commerce site while raising awareness about sustainable choices.

Scenarios

  • Which of these products are made from organic or recycled materials?
  • How can I ensure the clothing I buy aligns with my sustainability values?
